Payments App — Release Recovery

Triage, replanned, and delivered a sequence of mobile releases that had stalled. Brought predictability back to the roadmap.

Role: Product lead Timeline: 10 weeks Teams: Product, Eng, QA
Product Mobile Execution
Work together Back to work

Situation

Two planned releases slipped repeatedly, trust was low, and defects were piling up.

  • No single view of risk across squads
  • Scope creep and unclear exit criteria
  • Regulatory requirements at risk of missing dates

Approach

Reset the plan and governance to make outcomes visible and achievable.

  • Created a three-release sequence (stabilize → compliance → growth)
  • Instituted weekly risk reviews and daily defect burn-down
  • Aligned design, engineering, and QA on Definition of Done
  • Moved leadership updates to short, metric-driven briefs

Toolkit

  • Roadmap & scope board with clear owners
  • Risk RAID log tied to Jira epics
  • Release checklists for App Store / Play Store
  • Crash monitoring + regression suite

Outcomes

  • Shipped three consecutive releases on the reset cadence
  • Crash-free sessions improved after defect burn-down
  • Stakeholders regained predictability on compliance milestones
