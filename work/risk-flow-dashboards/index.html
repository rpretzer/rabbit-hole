Risk & Flow Dashboards

Created a single source of truth for delivery health, risk, and flow so decisions weren’t made from anecdotes.

Role: Product + delivery Timeline: 5 weeks Users: Leadership + teams
Product Data Delivery
Work together Back to work

Situation

Leaders lacked a trustworthy view of throughput and risk across squads.

  • Different teams measured “done” differently
  • No place to see blockers, dependencies, or burn-down
  • Updates were long emails instead of a live dashboard

Approach

Started with the questions leadership actually asked, then answered them in one place.

  • Defined common metrics: cycle time, WIP, blocked items, risk owners
  • Instrumented Jira and Git data into a clean model
  • Built a simple UI with filters for teams, releases, and risk type
  • Added weekly snapshot exports for exec briefings

What mattered

  • Clear ownership of each risk with due dates
  • Visible bottlenecks by stage so teams could swarm
  • Trends over time, not just a point-in-time board
  • No new tooling burden on teams

Outcomes

  • Weekly leadership check-ins used the dashboard instead of slides
  • Risks had owners and due dates, reducing surprises late in the cycle
  • Teams could see where work was stuck and self-correct faster
