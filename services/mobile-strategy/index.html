The Mobile Strategy Challenge

Enterprise mobile development often struggles with fragmented strategy, competing priorities, and the complexity of coordinating multiple teams toward shared goals.

Disconnected Roadmaps

Multiple teams building mobile features without unified strategy, leading to inconsistent user experiences and duplicated effort.

Resource Misalignment

Development capacity focused on tactical features rather than strategic initiatives that drive business outcomes.

Platform Fragmentation

iOS and Android implementations diverging in functionality and user experience due to lack of coordinated product ownership.

Stakeholder Conflicts

Business units with competing mobile priorities and no clear framework for prioritization and trade-off decisions.
