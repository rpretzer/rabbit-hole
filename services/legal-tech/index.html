Case Study: Progressive Insurance

The Challenge

Progressive Insurance needed to streamline legal review processes for state filing requirements across all 50 states. Each state had unique regulatory requirements creating bottlenecks in product development and marketing campaigns.

The Solution

Designed collaborative workflow that integrated legal review into content creation process rather than treating it as separate approval gate. Built early-warning system that flagged potential legal issues during content creation.

The Innovation

Shifted from reactive approval to proactive guidance, preventing legal issues before they required formal review. This fundamental change in approach transformed legal from gatekeeper to enabler.
