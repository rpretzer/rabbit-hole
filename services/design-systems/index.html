Systems That Scale With Organizations

People-First Design Systems

Enterprise design systems succeed when they solve people problems, not just design problems. After leading system initiatives at USAA and Progressive, I've learned that adoption comes through value creation, not mandates.

  • Start with high-pain teams to create early advocates
  • Build components that make developers' jobs easier
  • Create governance through collaboration, not control
  • Measure relationship health alongside compliance metrics
  • Design for organic adoption and community contribution

Case Study: USAA Mobile Design System

Leading product management for enterprise mobile design system across 50+ development teams

The Challenge

USAA needed to coordinate mobile development across 50+ teams while maintaining consistency and accelerating feature delivery for their financial services platform serving 13+ million members.

The Approach

Led product management for enterprise mobile design system, managing roadmap and backlog through PI planning process. Established shared components, development patterns, and adoption strategies.

The Innovation

Created advocacy-driven adoption model where early adopter teams became champions, spreading system usage through peer influence rather than top-down mandates.

Design System Development Process

A proven methodology for building design systems that teams actually want to use, based on Fortune 500 experience.

1

Discovery & Assessment

Audit current design and development practices, identify pain points, and establish system requirements with stakeholder interviews and technical assessment.

Deliverables: Assessment report, system requirements, success metrics
2

Foundation & Architecture

Establish design tokens, component architecture, and governance model. Create pilot components with high-pain early adopter teams.

Deliverables: Design tokens, component library foundation, governance framework
3

Core Library Development

Build essential components with proper documentation, testing, and developer tools. Focus on components that solve the biggest problems first.

Deliverables: Core component library, documentation site, developer tools
4

Adoption & Scale

Roll out system through advocacy-driven adoption, provide training and support, establish feedback loops and continuous improvement processes.

Deliverables: Training materials, support processes, community guidelines
